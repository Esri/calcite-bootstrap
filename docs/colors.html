Colors

All colors on this page have been tested for web accessibility color contrast requirements with black and white. AA indicates that the color passes for large text in that combination. aa indicates the color passes for regular text in that combination. To check contrast with combinations other than black and white, use webaim.org.

AA - Large text is the equivalent of 14px bold or greater or 18px regular or greater
aa - Regular text is less than 14px bold or less than 18px regular
